Although he only has a cameo role, the fox is a super-duper important character in this book. He teaches the prince lessons that the prince then teaches the narrator (and by extension us, the readers.)

The fox rescues the little prince from the despair he had fallen into on seeing a garden full of roses on Earth—until that point, the prince had believed his flower (who was also a rose) when she told him that she was the only one of her kind in the universe.

But after the prince tames him, the fox tells him to go again to see the roses in the garden, and that this time, the prince will see that his rose is indeed unique.

This is little prince

The little prince’s home is a tiny asteroid named B-612, which is no bigger than a house.

The little prince represents the open-mindedness of children. He is a wanderer who restlessly asks questions and is willing to engage the invisible, secret mysteries of the universe. The novel suggests that such inquisitiveness is the key to understanding and to happiness.

The website’s educational content is about mental health. Mental health is often ignored by people. Many people do not seek help when they experience symptoms of mental illness, maybe because they don’t know how to request for help, or they don’t even realize that they have mental health problems. Therefore, I would use “The Little Prince” as the story theme for website to raise the awareness for mental health. The website will provide a broad understanding of mental health to users, introduce the main kinds of mental health problems, ways to request for help, tips to maintain a good mental health and encourage people to seek help for mental health issues.
