Features

Know Yourself

Access the presence or absence of Depression, Anxiety or Stress by scoring the scale DASS 21. High scores on DASS certainly alert the user to a high level of stress, and this need be explored further by intervention process.

First Aid

The first aid intervention process composed by four treatment videos. By watching the videos, you are able to understand the present situation and treat the stress by release panic feeling, then receive the further assistance.

Meet Your Specialist

Once you have complete the first aid intervention process, the Mind Initiative system provides platform that connects you and specialist, clicking a booking button, you are able to meet with specialist no matter when and where.
